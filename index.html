Yamaha YZF-R6

The Yamaha YZF-R6, commonly refered to the R6 is a beast of a machine. Although having a smaller engine than the R1, the R6 generates high power at high speed and high RPM. Just because the engine looks smaller than the R1 doesn't mean it lacks capacity. The R6 has an astonishing redline of 16,500 rpm and delivers a massive 117 horsepower at 14,500 rpm. This allows it to travel at a speed of over 160 miles an hour, while maintaining perfect cornering capabilities.
